Devil Fruits

Devil Fruits are mysterious, legendary fruits scattered across the world, each granting a unique and extraordinary power in exchange for the ability to swim. There are roughly 100–200 known Devil Fruits, though their true origins remain buried in history. They are divided into three types: Paramecia, Zoan, and Logia, and no two Devil Fruits share the same ability. Their powers can reshape battles, nations, and even the fate of the world, making them some of the most sought-after and dangerous treasures on the seas.

Gomu Gomu no Mi / Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Sun God Nika


User: Monkey D. Luffy Originally believed to be a simple rubber fruit, it is actually a mythical Zoan tied to Nika, the legendary warrior of liberation. Its awakening unlocks Gear 5 — limitless freedom, cartoon-like power, and the “Drums of Liberation,” connecting Luffy to Joy Boy’s will. This fruit represents rebellion, joy, and the power to change the world.

Yami Yami no Mi (Dark-Dark Fruit)


User: Blackbeard A Logia unlike any other, instead of turning the user into an element, it lets them control darkness and gravity itself. It can absorb anything, nullify Devil Fruit powers on touch, and is considered the most dangerous power in existence. This fruit symbolizes destruction, ambition, and the darkness at the heart of the world.
